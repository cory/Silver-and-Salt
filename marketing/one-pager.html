Silver & Salt Capital

The idea is simple.

We invest in Utah women founders to make money.

When a group of women commits to work together, there's no broken system we can't fix. Let's fund Utah's overlooked founders and win together as women in Utah.

You're already investing. Learn how to do it better.

Silver & Salt Capital is an angel investment collective where accredited investors pool their capital to fund early-stage companies founded by women in Utah. We commit $10,000 a year for five years (a total of $50,000), vet deals together, and make our own individual investment decisions, building a diversified portfolio across 30+ companies. The model is simple and is working in other parts of the country.

The gap in Utah startups founded by women is the investment opportunity. We are seeking 100 women to join as founding members.

If you believe Utah women can do more together, this is the place for you.
