We are creating regenerative capital in Utah.

When one investor writes a check, a founder gets funded. When a hundred do it together — sourcing, vetting, mentoring, connecting — the entire ecosystem shifts.

Earmark capital for women founders and more women will build companies. More companies mean better deal flow. Better deal flow means better returns. The cycle accelerates.

Our power is in the number of investors now working in parallel to meaningfully fund the overall startup ecosystem. This is smart capital, deployed where it performs best.

Every member is building the system our daughters will inherit.

Why Silver & Salt?

This is the place and
these are our resources.

Utah is one of the best places in America to build a business1 — and still one of the hardest places to be a woman building one.2

The problem is not talent. The problem is not ambition. The problem is participation and access to growth capital. This group unites women in the Utah region with a shared purpose: to help new women founders launch and lead successful businesses in our community.

Capital exists here. Experience exists here. Influence exists here. We know that despite all of this, many women remain outside the networks where investment decisions are made.

Silver & Salt Capital represent two minerals that shaped the Park City and Wasatch region. At times, they've both been more valuable than gold, and they represent the resources we have to shape a new landscape for women founders today.

And yes, if we have a few silver hairs or are just a little more salty than one might expect, it's all a part of what makes our Utah community so strong and vibrant.

Who We Are

An angel investor writes checks to early-stage companies. We're building a community of them.

Don't you know?
I'm an angel.

See the full landscape of women's angel collectives →

Silver & Salt Capital is a coordinated community of individual investors who learn together, evaluate opportunities together, and invest together — while maintaining individual choice.

We are executives, operators, entrepreneurs, professionals, and community leaders who are no longer waiting for invitations into existing systems. We are rewriting how women participate in economic power and building the systems we need for our capital to thrive.

Across the country, women's angel collectives like ours are already thriving. We're not new, just new to Utah. From Golden Seeds in New York (290 members, $190M+ invested over 20 years) to The JumpFund in Chattanooga (100+ investors, 19× co-investment leverage).4
