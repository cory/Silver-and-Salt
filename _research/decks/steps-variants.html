Three steps. Three vertical takes.

Same content, three design directions. Pick your favorite, or mix and match.

Variant A
Editorial cards

Soft sand cards with a lime hairline. Hover lifts the card. Calm, scannable, gentle motion.

  1. 01

    Pool the check

    100 investors each write a $1,000 check and a Utah women founder gets startup funding for $100,000.

  2. 02

    Spread the risk

    As investors, we are going to spread out our investment risk by investing in 30 founders over 5 years. ($10,000 investment commitment each year).

  3. 03

    Show up

    Support, shop, and advocate for our founders and the companies we invest in. This is where we live our values with our money and wait for the returns.
