Session Workflow

How to work with Claude on this repo
Every session — three steps
1
Your repo is already cloned to ~/Documents/SilverAndSalt. Claude mounts it automatically. No downloading needed — just open Cowork and Claude has the latest files.
2
Tell Claude what you want to work on. Claude reads the repo, picks up where you left off, and edits files directly. Reference this page, any page, or any agent by name.
3
Push when Claude is done. In Terminal: cd ~/Documents/SilverAndSalt && git push origin main — your site at silverandsaltcapital.com updates in about 60 seconds.

Split Circle Logo — All 7 Rules Locked · Verify before publishing any mark
  • 1Split is always 50/50 vertical — never diagonal, horizontal, or off-center. Use clip-path or absolute-positioned halves. Never CSS gradients at angles.
  • 2Must include silver or white in the ampersand or one half.
  • 3Colors may include moss (#2F3E34). Will NOT include cream.
  • 4Moss may match the background — one half can disappear.
  • 5Only one accent color per version. All other elements are silver, white, or moss.
  • 6The & is always white, silver, or moss — NEVER an accent color. When an accent is present elsewhere, the & MUST be white or silver.
  • 7The & must be a different color from both halves — never matches either side.
⭐ Favorites: Moss/Accent + White & · Silver/Accent + White & · Moss/Silver + White & · Silver/White + Moss &
