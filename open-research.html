Our Methodology
Silver & Salt Capital uses AI tools — primarily Claude (Anthropic) — to assist with research synthesis and analysis. AI is a starting point, not an endpoint. Every significant claim is verified against primary sources before publication. We name the tools we use. We publish our source lists. We flag estimates as estimates.
Show the work
Methodology is documented in every report. You can see exactly how we got here.
Credit the tools
AI tools are named and described. No pretending this is traditional research.
Link the sources
Every primary source is cited. Our full Source Bible is available to members.
