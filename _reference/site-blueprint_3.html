Session Workflow

How to use these files with Claude
Every session — three steps
1
Download your files from GitHub. Go to your silver-and-salt-capital repo, click the file, click the download icon. Do this for: site-blueprint.html, source-bible.html, ecosystem-map.html.
2
Upload all three to Claude at the start of your session. Say: "Here are my three files — let's work on [page or task]." Claude reads them and picks up exactly where you left off.
3
At the end of your session, upload the updated files back to GitHub. Go to your repo → click the file → click the pencil (edit) icon → paste the new code → click "Commit changes." Nothing is ever lost.

Split Circle Logo — All 7 Rules Locked · Verify against all 7 before publishing any mark
  • 1Split is always 50/50 vertical — never diagonal, horizontal, or off-center. Use clip-path or absolute-positioned halves. Never CSS gradients at angles.
  • 2Must include silver or white in the ampersand or one half.
  • 3Colors may include moss (#2F3E34). Will NOT include cream.
  • 4Moss may match the background — one half can disappear.
  • 5Only one accent color per version. All other elements are silver, white, or moss.
  • 6The & is always white, silver, or moss — NEVER an accent color. When an accent is present elsewhere in the mark, the & MUST be white or silver (never moss).
  • 7The & must be a different color from both halves — never matches either side.
Logo Preferences
⭐ Favorites (use first): Moss/Accent + White & · Silver/Accent + White & · Moss/Silver + White & · Silver/White + Moss &
Acceptable: Moss/Accent + Silver & · White/Accent + Silver & · Moss/White + Silver &
Note: White-half variants lose edge on white backgrounds — restrict to dark or cream surfaces.
