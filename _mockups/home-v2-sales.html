Who can join?

Silver & Salt Capital is for accredited investors. That's a legal term — it means you earn enough or have enough savings that the government says you can invest in startups (companies that aren't on the stock market yet).

Income of $200K+ annually ($300K with a spouse)
Or net worth over $1M, excluding your primary home
Or certain professional certifications (Series 7, 65, 82)

Not sure if you qualify? We can help you figure that out.
