Below are seven candidates rendered with the actual numerals from the 2025 Utah Funding snapshot. The headline sample is the same in each card so you can judge editorial register alongside legibility.

How to read this page.

Each card uses the candidate font only for the headline sample, the four-glyph row at the top, and the snapshot numerals. All other text on the page is Satoshi (body) or Cormorant Garamond (intro headline). That isolates exactly what would change in a sitewide swap.

The first card is the current state — there for comparison. Look at the 1 in each four-glyph row first, then the headline sample, then the small "$1.25M · $6M" line where digits sit next to letters at body size. If the candidate fails any of those, it is not the answer.

Pick a winner and I will update styles.css and the snapshot CSS in one pass, then verify on mobile and desktop.
