How It Works

Simple. We find great founders. You pick who to invest in.

No fund manager decides for you. No blind pool. You see every deal, you do your own homework (with our help), and you write your own checks.

Step 1
We find them
Our team finds women founders in Utah who are building strong companies. We study each one carefully before we share it with you.
Step 2
You pick
We show you what we found. You read the reports. You ask questions. Then you decide which companies to put your money into. Every choice is yours.
Step 3
You grow
Over five years, you build a collection of 30+ investments. You own a small piece of many companies — that's how the best angel investors succeed.

The Power of Together

One check won't change a company. One hundred might change an industry.

You invest $1,500 in a founder you believe in. So do 79 other women. Alone, that's a small check. Together, it's $120,000 — and a founder's next chapter.

This is what makes a group different from investing alone. No single person writes a check big enough to matter. But when a hundred women invest together, every founder in the pipeline gets the capital she needs to grow. Your small bet becomes part of something much bigger.

Your Money, Your Decisions

This is not a fund. It's your portfolio.

At Silver & Salt Capital, no one invests your money for you. We find the opportunities. We do the research. But every dollar you invest is your choice.

You write your own checks. You pick your own companies. You build your own portfolio over time.

Think of it this way: We're the research team. You're the decision-maker.

Who can join? Accredited investors.

"Accredited investor" is a legal term. It just means you meet certain income or wealth thresholds that allow you to invest in startups — companies that aren't on the stock market yet.

Income $200K+/year on your own, or $300K+ with a spouse
Net worth Over $1M, not counting your home
Credentials Certain financial licenses (Series 7, 65, or 82)

Not sure if you qualify? That's okay. Reach out and we'll help.

The Math Behind It

More companies = better odds.

Most startups fail. That's normal. But if you invest in enough of them, the winners more than make up for the losses. That's why we spread your money across many companies, not just one or two.

Investors who spread money across 50+ companies earned a median return of 14% per year. Those with fewer than 5 investments? Zero. We help you build that diversified portfolio.

Source: Correlation Ventures · Wiltbank & Boeker, Angel Capital Association
