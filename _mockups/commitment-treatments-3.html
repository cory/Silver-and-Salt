Our Community Commitment
"Every woman in Utah deserves to feel she is enough."

We are a group of women who commit to standing as fierce advocates for one another.

We imagine and build new companies in proximity with one another to support the communities we need to thrive.

We Commit
To showing up — with capital, with knowledge, with relationships, with visibility, with advocacy.
To leading with curiosity & assuming positive intent.
To being kind & being effective.
To amplifying each other's voices & operating from abundance, not scarcity.
Because we hold this truth:
Utah's next generation of innovation will be shaped by founders & determined by funders — it's time more of them are women.
