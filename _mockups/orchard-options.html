Orchard — Four Visual Directions

Same copy, four different design approaches for the WHAT section.

Option 01 · Editorial strip (four plates)
What it is

Think of it like investing in an orchard.

We plant lots of seeds and tend all of them so that some will bear fruit.

i Step 01 · The seeds

We form a big group.

We all write small checks and collectively make big investments.

ii Step 02 · The commitment

$10,000 a year, for five years.

You know your number up front. No surprises.

iii Step 03 · Tended together

We back every founder we fund.

Research, customers, network. The full group behind every company.

iv Step 04 · The harvest

Some will bear fruit.

Some will fail. Some will grow into real companies. That's where returns come from.
